## Featured Legendary Cols

These six cols represent the crown jewels of the RAID Alpine:

### 1. Cime de la Bonette (2,802m) - Day 5
- **The highest paved road in Europe**
- 23km climb at 6.5% average, 10.5% max
- First crossed: 1962
- 6 Tour de France appearances

### 2. Col de l'Iseran (2,770m) - Day 3
- **Highest paved pass in the Alps**
- Epic 36km climb at 4.5% average
- First crossed: 1938
- 9 Tour de France appearances

### 3. Col du Galibier (2,642m) - Day 4
- **Most iconic climb in cycling history**
- 18.1km at 6.9% average, brutal 10.1% final km
- First crossed: 1911
- 61 Tour de France appearances (most of any col on this route)

### 4. Col d'Izoard (2,361m) - Day 4
- **Famous Casse Déserte moonscape**
- Monuments to Coppi and Bobet
- First crossed: 1922
- 35 Tour de France appearances

### 5. Cormet de Roselend (1,968m) - Day 2
- **Most scenic climb in the Alps**
- Stunning turquoise reservoir views
- 19.9km at 6.1% average
- 7 Tour de France appearances

### 6. Col de la Colombiere (1,613m) - Day 1
- **Beautiful introduction to Alpine climbing**
- Consistent gradients through alpine meadows
- 11.8km at 5.9% average
- 22 Tour de France appearances
